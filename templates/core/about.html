About MPTC Karunagappally

Pioneering Technical Education Since 1997

Our History

Model Polytechnic College Karunagappally was established in the year 1997. It is managed by the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), a Government of Kerala undertaking. The institution is affiliated with the Board of Technical Education, Kerala, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in technical education, nurturing competent professionals who can contribute to the technological and socioeconomic development of the society.

Mission

To provide quality education through innovative teaching-learning processes. To equip students with required skills to adapt to the changing global scenario.
