About the Institution

A Legacy of Technical Excellence

Model Polytechnic College Karunagappally, established in 1997, is a premier technical institution managed by the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), Government of Kerala.

Approved by AICTE and affiliated to the Board of Technical Education Kerala, we have been consistently producing skilled diploma engineers who excel in industry and higher education. Our modern labs, dedicated faculty, and strong industry connections set us apart.
