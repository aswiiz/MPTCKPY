About the Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell at Model Polytechnic College Karunagappally bridges students with top-tier companies through campus drives, internships, and comprehensive skill development programmes. Our dedicated team of coordinators works year-round to ensure every student has the opportunity to launch a rewarding career.

We partner with industry leaders and growing companies, offering students diverse career options in manufacturing, electronics, IT, and service sectors.

  • On-campus and off-campus recruitment drives
  • Aptitude, technical & soft skills training
  • Resume writing and mock interview sessions
  • Industry visits and internship facilitation
